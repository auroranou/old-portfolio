About this project

Civic Fix was my first project for General Assembly's Web Development Immersive. Inspired by the travails of living in the District, the app is meant to enhance civil society by providing a platform for users to post PSAs and interact with public officials.

The app allows users to email agencies directly with individual and community concerns. In the updated version, they can also tag locations on maps and sort posts by neighborhood.

The original iteration of this app was built in Ruby using a Sinatra framework. A Rails version of Civic Fix is currently in the works! View the original in action here and its source code here.
